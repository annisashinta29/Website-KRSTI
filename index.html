Barelang 7

A dance robot is a humanoid robot designed to be able to dance based on traditional Indonesian dance movements. In the Indonesian context, dance robots are known in the form of KRSTI (Indonesian Dance Robot Contest) which participates in the KRI (Indonesian Robot Contest) competition every year. This dance robot consists of 2 humanoid robots that can dance based on cultural dance movements in Indonesia. This robot can dance with musical accompaniment, performing the movements needed according to the KRSTI theme.

History of KRSTI

The Indonesian Dance Robot Contest (KRSTI) is a competition for the design, manufacture and programming of robots accompanied by elements of Indonesian art and culture, especially the art of dance that has been famous on earth. KRSTI (which previously named KRSI - Indonesian Robot Art Contest) was first held in 2010 with the theme ‘Robot Jaipong Dancer’. Furthermore, in 2019 with the theme ‘Robot dancer Jaipong Kembang Tanjung’, in 2020 with the theme ‘Robot dancer Engang’, in 2021 with the theme ‘Robot dancer Gambyong Pareanom, in 2022 with the theme “Robot dancer Kancet Ledo”. Kancet Ledo’, and in 2023 with the theme “Denok Semarang Dance”.

Each team consists of 3 (three) active students and an active supervisor from the same institution. institution. Each participant team is required to create two coordinated robots to perform a dance that reflects Indonesian culture according to the contest theme.

KRSTI 2024

For KRSTI 2024, again to arouse the love and preservation of dance and national cultures, the theme raised is ‘Robot Dancer Oleg Tamulilingan Peliangan style’ from Bali. This KRSTI 2024 activity was held in conjunction with the implementation of the Indonesian Robot Contest (KRI) at the Regional level and KRI at the National level. The purpose of this robot contest is to foster the creativity and interest of students in the development of technology, especially in the field of robotics, which is intended for industry and cultural arts. for industry and cultural arts, especially dance.
